# Executive Findings

## What I analyzed

I joined workbook inventory, load-time samples, extract refreshes, support tickets, and KPI definitions to rank Tableau governance risk.

## Findings

- The dataset contains 28 workbook assets, 3,200 performance samples, 2,520 extract runs, 720 support tickets, and 64 KPI definitions.
- 37 KPI definitions are not certified, which is a clear publishing and stakeholder-support risk.
- The highest-risk workbook is WB016 (Sales Scorecard) with 26 support tickets and 33.7s average load time.

## Recommendation

Treat Tableau optimization as an operating workflow: reduce workbook QA rework, certify KPI definitions, and retire or schedule ad hoc workbooks before publishing more executive views.
